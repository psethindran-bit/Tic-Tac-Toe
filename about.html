How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Modes:

  • Human vs Human: Take turns with a friend
  • Easy AI: Perfect for beginners, makes random moves
  • Medium AI: Balanced gameplay with strategic thinking
  • Impossible AI: Uses perfect play algorithm—can you force a draw?
